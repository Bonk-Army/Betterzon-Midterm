1.1.1 Our Vision


Once upon a time there was a plethora of shops.
Today we say "Alexa, order toilet paper"
There is no choice, there is no thought.

We want to make it easy to order from different shops in a simple and "better" way.
We want to make Betterzon.

1.1.2 Our Idea


  • gather data from Online Shops
  • show customers that there are offers
    • that are cheaper than amazon
    • nearby
    • or both!

In order to achieve that goal, we want to

  • create a webcrawler that collects information
  • give local shop owners the possibility to participate
